About Toasty's Cafe

After years of serving loyal patrons of Seattle, JB diner sold their space to five coding students at Code Fellows and hence the Toasty's Cafe was born. Shortly after the opening, the Toasty's Cafe experienced a sudden death to one of the founders. From the sudden loss of a partner, came a progress to a wonderful cafe in tribute to their partner and remodeling of the restaurant.

For a classic, hearty American breakfast food, come to Toasty's Cafe! Since 2021, we've been serving up legendary breakfast food all over the world! Our famous chicken and waffles won't leave you hungry for the entire day! Our cafe has been featured on the Travel Channel, the Food Network, and was even voted by the Seattle Times as the "Best Place to Cure a Hangover"
